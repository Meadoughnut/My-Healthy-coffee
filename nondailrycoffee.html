    This will contain five cards for five different drinks that elaborate about the drink and component (ingredient).

    Almond brown sugar coffee

    1. - 4 pumps of torani brown sugar syrup
    2. - 2 decafs shot of espresso
    3. - Add the oat milk to half cup
    4. - Add ice accordingly.

    soy hot matcha latte

    1. - Pour the soy milk to the 16oz of the milk pitcher
    2. - Add 2 scoop of matcha powder.
    3. - Steam together.

    Tough Caramel Almond ice coffee topped with non-dairy milk

    1. - Add 1 decaf shot of espresso in the cup
    2. - Add 3 pumps of the Torani caramel syrup
    3. - Add ice
    4. -- Add a splash of Almond milk
    5. - Add cinnamon on top

    vanilla coconut Ice blended

    1. Pour ¼ cup of coconut milk in your blender.
    2. Add one scoop of ice
    3. - 4 pumps of torani syrup
    4. 3 pumps of crème syrup
    5. Blend it.
    6. cinnamon on top

    vanilla oat milk latte

    1. - Add 3 pumps of vanilla in your cup
    2. Pour 1 decaf shot of espresso in the cup
    3. steam the oatmilk
    4. pour the oatmilk in the mixed cup
