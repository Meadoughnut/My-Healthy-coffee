    Frequently Asked Questions

    Where can I get the syrups?

    I recommend using Torani brand syrups for their quality. According to Torani's website, they use sucralose and acesulfame potassium (aka Ace-K) to add sweetness to their sugar-free syrups. You can order directly from their website https://shop.torani.com/?gad_source=1&gclid=Cj0KCQjw-ai0BhDPARIsAB6hmP4GKYbEsmEkrWw48OLf9hbfv-f33RuMnm0Qyl0711VEQOnXlfks8a0aAtuTEALw_wcB or from https://www.amazon.com

    Can I use different types of milk?

    Absolutely! While the website caters to specific preferences, you are welcome to use your preferred milk. For non-dairy options, there are five popular choices: coconut milk, almond milk, oat milk, soy milk, and pistachio milk. Each brings a unique flavor and texture to your coffee

    How many pumps of syrup should we use for different cup sizes?

    For a 12oz cup, I recommend using no more than 3 pumps of syrup. For a 16oz cup, use up to 4 pumps, and for larger cups, around 26-30oz, use no more than 7 pumps. Adjusting the number of pumps helps maintain the perfect balance of sweetness and flavor.
