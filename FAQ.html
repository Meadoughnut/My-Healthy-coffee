Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ethiopian cuisine?

Ethiopian cuisine consists of a variety of flavorful dishes, typically centered around injera, a type of sourdough flatbread, and accompanied by spicy stews and vegetables.

Is Ethiopian food spicy?

Many Ethiopian dishes are spicy, as they use a blend of spices called berbere. However, there are also milder options available.

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes, Ethiopian cuisine offers a wide variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes, making it a great choice for those with dietary preferences.

Can I find gluten-free options?

Injera, the staple bread in Ethiopian cuisine, is traditionally made from teff, which is naturally gluten-free. However, it's important to confirm with the restaurant to ensure it meets gluten-free standards.
