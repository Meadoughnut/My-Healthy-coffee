This will contain five cards for five different drinks that elaborate about the drink and component (ingredient).

Almond sugar-free brown sugar coffee blend

  1. Pour ¼ cup of coconut milk in your blender.
  2. Add one scoop of ice.
  3. 4 pumps of Torani sugar-free vanilla syrup
  4. 3 pumps of crème syrup
  5. Blend it.

Tough sugar-free Caramel Almond ice coffee topped with non-dairy milk

  1. Add 1 decaf shot of espresso in the cup
  2. Add 3 pumps of the Torani sugar-free caramel syrup
  3. Add ice
  4. Add a splash of Almond milk
  5. Add cinnamon on top

soy sugar-free Hazelnut hot matcha latte

  1. Pour the soy milk to the 16oz of the milk pitcher
  2. Add 2 scoop of matcha powder.
  3. Steam together.
  4. -Add 2 pumps of sugar-free hazelnut torani syrup
  5. -Pour the milk inside and stir it.

sugar-free vanilla coconut Ice blended

  1. Pour ¼ cup of coconut milk in your blender.
  2. Add one scoop of ice
  3. 4 pumps of torani sugar-free vanilla syrup
  4. 3 pumps of crème syrup
  5. 1 shot of decaf espressoo
  6. Blend it.
  7. line up the cup with caramel sauce
  8. top up with whip cream

sugar-free Irish creme oat milk shaken with pistachio

  1. Add 3 pumps of sugar-free Irish crème in your shaker
  2. Add one scoop of ice
  3. Pour 1 decaf shot of espresso
  4. Add cinnamon powder
  5. Shake it well.
  6. Before pouring the shaken
  7. Pour oat milk in the shaker
  8. Add one 4 teaspoons of Pistachio nut
  9. Shake it well
  10. Add the shaken oat in the cup
  11. Ended up by adding the shaken decaf in the cup.
  12. Top with cinnamon and pistachio nut.
